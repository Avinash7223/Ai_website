About Ai

AI (Artificial Intelligence) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines designed to think, learn, and solve problems like humans. It involves creating algorithms and models that enable computers to perform tasks such as reasoning, pattern recognition, decision-making, and natural language understanding. AI is categorized into three types:
Narrow AI Focuses on specific tasks (e.g., voice assistants, recommendation systems).
General AI Hypothetical AI that can perform any intellectual task a human can do.
Superintelligent AI A theoretical form of AI that exceeds human intelligence.
